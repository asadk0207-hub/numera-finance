Frequently Asked Questions

Are your calculators really free?

Yes! All our calculators are completely free to use with no hidden charges, no registration required, and no premium features.

How accurate are the calculations?

We use industry-standard financial formulas and regularly test our calculators to ensure accuracy. However, always consult professionals for important financial decisions.

Do you store my financial data?

No, all calculations are performed locally in your browser. We don't store or transmit any of your financial information.

Can I suggest new calculators?

Absolutely! We love user suggestions and regularly implement new calculators based on user feedback. Use the contact form above to share your ideas.

How often do you update the calculators?

We continuously monitor and update our calculators to ensure they reflect current financial regulations and best practices.

Can I use these calculators for business purposes?

Our calculators are for personal use and educational purposes. For business applications, please contact us to discuss your specific needs.
